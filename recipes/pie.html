Directions

  1. Mix the spices in a small bowl.

  2. Beat the eggs in a large bowl. Stir in the pumpkin and spices,

    then pour the filling into the unbaked pie crust.

  3. Bake the pie for about 15 minutes in the preheated oven,

    then reduce the heat and bake until a knife comes out clean.

  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
